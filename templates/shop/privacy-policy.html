Privacy Policy

At MyCure360, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information Collection

We collect personal information, including:

  • Your name, email, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Medical history and treatment preferences (only as provided by you).
  • Payment details when booking medical treatments or accommodations.
  • Travel and accommodation preferences, if applicable.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate medical consultations and treatment bookings.
  • Assist in travel and accommodation arrangements.
  • Improve customer service and provide personalized recommendations.
  • Send important updates, appointment reminders, and promotional offers.

Third-Party Services

We may share your personal information with:

  • Hospitals, clinics, and doctors for treatment facilitation.
  • Travel and accommodation partners for booking assistance.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@mycure360.com.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
